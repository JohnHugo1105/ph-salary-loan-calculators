Local Storage & Calculation Data

When you use our calculators or the "Save Calculation" feature, your financial inputs and results are processed and stored strictly locally on your device using your browser's Local Storage. We do not transmit, collect, or store any of your personal financial data on our servers.

When you use the "Generate Share Link" feature, your inputs are mathematically encoded directly into the URL link. This allows you to share your computation with others without us ever saving the data to a database.
