Our Mission

PhCalculators.com is a free tool built by Filipino developers to help employees, freelancers, and business owners in the Philippines compute their accurate net pay, government contributions (SSS, PhilHealth, Pag-IBIG), and loan amortizations.

We focus on clarity, speed, and transparency. Unlike other tools that hide the logic, we aim to show the formulas and assumptions used, so you can trust the numbers.

check_circle
Accuracy & Sources

Our calculators use commonly referenced public guidance and official tables (e.g., from BIR, SSS, PhilHealth, and Pag-IBIG circulars).

Disclaimer: Actual employer policies or official tables may differ slightly. Always verify with official government sources or your HR department.
