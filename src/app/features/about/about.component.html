About PH Salary & Loan Calculators

PH Calculators helps workers, HR, and small businesses in the Philippines quickly estimate contributions (SSS, PhilHealth, Pag-IBIG, MPF), salary net pay, taxes, 13th month, and loan amortization. This site focuses on clarity and transparency, showing assumptions and formulas used by the tools.

Ownership & Author: This project is maintained by a software engineer based in the Philippines. Contact details are available on the Contact page. We welcome feedback, corrections, and requests for additional calculators.

Accuracy & Sources: Calculators use commonly referenced rules and public guidance (e.g., BIR, SSS, DOLE). Actual employer policies or official tables may differ. Always verify with official sources.

Monetization: We plan to support the site with unobtrusive ads to keep it free. We do not sell personal data. See our Privacy Policy and Disclaimer.
