Understanding your Contributions (2025 Updates)

Every month, portions of your salary are deducted for government-mandated benefits. These ensure you have access to social insurance, health coverage, and housing loans.

SSS Contribution Table 2025

The Social Security System (SSS) schedule has updated contribution rates. For 2025, the total contribution rate is 14%, shared by the employer (9.5%) and employee (4.5%). The Worker's Investment and Savings Program (WISP) is a mandatory provident fund for members with a Monthly Salary Credit (MSC) exceeding ₱20,000. It effectively increases your retirement savings.

PhilHealth Premium Rate

The Universal Health Care Law mandates a scheduled increase in premium rates. For 2025, the premium is set at 5.0% of the basic monthly income. The income floor is ₱10,000, and the ceiling has increased to ₱90,000 (or as determined by pending circulars).

Pag-IBIG & MPF

Home Development Mutual Fund (Pag-IBIG): The maximum monthly compensation used for computation is currently pegged at ₱5,000, resulting in a standard ₱100 employee contribution.
Mandatory Provident Fund (SSS MPF): Often confused with Pag-IBIG MP2, the SSS MPF (WISP) is automatic for high-income earners. It is separate from the regular social security pension.

Notes: SSS values use an approximation (14% rate split 4.5%/9.5% plus EC). PhilHealth uses 5% with floor 10,000 and ceiling 90,000.
